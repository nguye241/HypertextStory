This is a very short uninteresting story, but it's almost spooky season so why not?
 

It was Lunar New Year in Vietnam a few years ago, I went to a famous pagoda/temple to pray with my relatives.

After my cousin and I were done praying, we decided to walk around the yard looking around and admiring Buddist scultures. They were very grand and majestic, I could see the monks take very good care of this place.

We were walking for about ten minutes until we reached a small praying area with a smaller temple.

The place was built in a small courtyard a little bit far away from the main yard, we decided to pray there as well, for New Year's good luck.

After we were done with praying (again), on our way back to the main yard, we walked past a pretty big tree. That was the moment when *that* happened.

I was walking with my cousin, talking like usual, then suddenly I felt something like a cold hand grabbed my left shoulder.

I looked back.

There was no one there.

I ignored it, thinking it was just my imagination. But then, it happened again, and I heard a faint voice calling my name.

I turned to my cousin and asked "Did you say something?"

She didn't.

To be honest, I do not believe in the paranormal, but I did feel weird and freaked out a bit.

"We should go back, quick." I said to my cousin.

We walked away from there, and our lives were back to normal.

However, I still think about that moment sometimes, and I still can't explain what happened there.

And that was a brief spooky moment in my life, so far.
