A spooky momment that happened that one time part 2


I was walking with my cousin, talking like usual, then suddenly I felt something like a cold hand grabbed my left shoulder.

I looked back.

There was no one there.

I ignored it, thinking it was just my imagination. But then, it happened again, and I heard a faint voice calling my name.

I turned to my cousin and asked "Did you say something?"

She didn't.
