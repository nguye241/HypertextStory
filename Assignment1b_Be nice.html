This is a very random and unorganized rant, just my random thoughts on being nice to people.
 

I am a digital artist who is lucky enough to build a small community of people that appreciate my silly artwork. It was just a fun hobby that I decided to share with some people online who might enjoy the same media.

At first, I was sharing my silly doodles randomly, no one saw them but this little hobby makes me happy, since I love drawing.

But then some people who eventually saw them, liked them and commented "I love your art, it makes me feel happy."

After reading that comment, I felt warm inside, I felt happy, since I never expected my silly hobby can make someone's day. They are just silly drawings, but it can make someone smiles.

That was an online interaction. What about real life?

We all have one of those days, where we feel gloomy, cranky and just want to be alone. Sometimes we feel like the world hates us and everything just feels empty.

But it's not.

We have come across many people in our lives, and of course we don't get along and like every single person we meet.

But we don't have to make it a negative thing, we don't have to spend time with people we don't get along and think that everyone is like that.

Let's be brave and maybe say hi or ask someone who was waiting for the bus with you how their day is going so far. Try to meet new people.

Maybe they were having one of those days, but a simple gesture of caring might improve their day, might not be a lot, but just a little bit, and sometimes just a little bit is enough.

Just relax yourself a bit, a random nice gesture won't hurt. I promise.
